Install on Home Screen

2015-10-03

Track Tally is best used as an App installed on your home screen. Follow the steps below to install.

To get started, browse https://tracktally.github.io/ in your phone browser.

  • Install on Android
  • Install on iPhone
  • Join a Challenge

Install on Android

Click on the three dots (1) in your browser and choose “Add to Home Screen (2)”.

Install on iPhone

Follow the sequence 1 - 4 below to add the App to your Home Screen.

Join with Challenge ID

After having installed the App on your home screen, join a challenge with a Challenge ID or create a new challenge.

Launch the App.
